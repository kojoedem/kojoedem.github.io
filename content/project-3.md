**EDEMROBIN** is a lightweight IP address management tool built with **FastAPI, Tailwind CSS, and SQLite/Postgres**.  
It lets you upload Cisco or MikroTik router configs and automatically extracts IPs, VLANs, and NAT addresses‚Äîorganizing them into their respective IP blocks.
